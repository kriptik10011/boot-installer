# Privacy Policy

Weekly Review is a local-first desktop application. Your privacy is protected by design.

## Data Storage

- All data is stored locally on your computer at `%LOCALAPPDATA%\WeeklyReview`
- Your database is encrypted using SQLCipher (AES-256)
- No data is transmitted to any server, cloud service, or third party

## No Telemetry

- No analytics or usage tracking
- No crash reporting
- No network requests except those you explicitly initiate (e.g., importing a recipe from a URL)

## No Accounts

- Your PIN and account exist only on your local machine
- There is no remote authentication, no email, no phone number

## Data You Control

- **Export**: Back up your data via Settings at any time
- **Delete**: Uninstalling the app or deleting `%LOCALAPPDATA%\WeeklyReview` removes all data
- **Portability**: Your encrypted database file can be copied to another machine

## Third-Party Services

The only external network request the app makes is when you use the **recipe import** feature to fetch a recipe from a URL. This request goes directly to the website you specify. No data is sent to Weekly Review developers or any intermediary.

## Questions

For privacy questions, open an issue on the [GitHub repository](../../issues).
